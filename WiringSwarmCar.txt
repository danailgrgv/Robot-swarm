Robot Wiring

Wheels
	Vcc (red wire)
	Ground (black wire)
	Signal (white wire)	D13(PA5), A1(PA1)
	Feeback (yellow wire)	PB14, PB15
Ultrasoon sensor (Pins are desribed when car is on it's wheels, looking straight at the sensor)
	Vcc (right pin)
	Ground (left pin)
	Trigger (2nd right pin)	D10(PB6)
	Echo (2nd left pin)	D5(PB4)
